Gaming Backlog Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on the Gaming Backlog Power-Up.

Information we collect

Google Analytics

Gaming Backlog uses Google Analytics, which stores cookies on your computer. The data that is collected includes your IP address and the time of your visit.

This information is not shared with third parties.

If you wish to opt out of Google Analytics, there is a browser add-on that will allow you to do so.

Your Trello data

Gaming Backlog requires usage of Trello's API to gain data about the names of your lists (so that they can be shown in the UI) as well as the ability to add cards to your board

Your Steam data

After logging in with Steam, Gaming Backlog securely stores your SteamID. This ID is used to get Steam game data if your profile is public.

Your consent

By using the Gaming Backlog Power-Up, you consent to our privacy policy.

Contact us

For questions regarding this privacy policy, please email hello@emgoto.com.

Changes
